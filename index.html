Discover Atlantica

I apologize for any confusion, but "Mahabodhi Gaya" typically refers to the same place as "Bodh Gaya." The Mahabodhi Temple, located in Bodh Gaya, Bihar, India, is one of the most significant and revered Buddhist pilgrimage sites in the world. This is where Siddhartha Gautama, who later became known as the Buddha, is believed to have attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree. I provided information about Mahabodhi Temple in my previous responses. If you have any specific questions or need more information about Mahabodhi Temple in Bodh Gaya, please feel free to ask, and I'll be happy to provide further details.
